Fun Facts About Babies 👶

Babies are more remarkable than you might think!

🧠

Brain Growth

A baby's brain nearly doubles in size during the first year, forming over 1 million new neural connections every single second.

😴

Sleep Time

Newborns sleep up to 17 hours a day. Their sleep cycles are shorter than adults — about 50 minutes — which is why they wake so often.

👂

Voice Recognition

Babies begin recognizing their mother's voice while still in the womb and can distinguish it from other voices just hours after birth.

🦴

More Bones

Babies are born with around 300 bones. As cartilage hardens and bones fuse over time, adults end up with only 206.

😊

First Smiles

Social smiling appears between 6 and 8 weeks as babies begin responding to familiar faces — your smile is their whole world.

👅

Taste Sensitivity

Newborns have more taste buds than adults — even on the roof and sides of the mouth — making them highly sensitive to flavor from day one.
